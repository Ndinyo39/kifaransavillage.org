Who We Are

Welcome To Kifaransa Village

The KIFARANSA VILLAGE Was founded in 2020 by Mr. Sammy SIMIYU and co- founders devoted to promote French language, encourage reciprocal communication between all levels of learning it and reuniting the french community and those who have intrest and passion to learn with benefits that Come with learning french. The Kifaransa Village was incorporated in 2022 and, after established opérations in Nairobi in patnership with Alliance Française , it extended its opérations to Coastal and Western/lake region adopting regional strategy under the regional exécutive officials in the other regions.
